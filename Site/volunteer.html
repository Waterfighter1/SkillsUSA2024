Volunteer Opportunities

Volunteer Requirements

  • Must attend orientation, hekd on the 3rd Thursday of each month
  • Must be 18 years of age, any minors MUST be accompanied by their parent or guardian (no exceptions)!
  • Must be mentored by an approved volunteer prior to your first time volunteering
  • Can follow all signs as directed by statff at the shelter

The shelter is open daily between 8am and 4pm for volunteers.

Volunteers are responsible for feeding, watering, and walking the animals. If you do not feel comfortable with a given animal, you do not have to care for it. Every animal will recieve care at some point in the day.

THANK YOU TO ALL NEW VOLUNTEERS WHO CAME OUT, CAN'T WAIT TO SEE YOU ALL AT THE SHELTER FOR LOTS OF VOLUNTEERING FUN!
